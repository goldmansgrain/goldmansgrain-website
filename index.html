About Goldman's Grain

Built on One Rule: Solid Hardwood Only.

Goldman's Grain was built on a simple rule — if it isn't solid hardwood, it doesn't leave the shop. No plywood. No MDF. No particle board held together by sawdust and optimism.

Every piece is made to order in Raleigh, NC by Brenden Goldman — cut, sanded, and finished by hand from maple, walnut, cherry, and oak sourced for their grain, their weight, and the way they look 20 years from now.
